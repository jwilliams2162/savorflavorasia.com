Kitchen Manager - Phoenix, AZ

Savor Flavor Asia is a new concept starting out in our first location. Currently our kitchen is mostly Chinese wok based but has some other Asian dishes and will be expanding into several Vietnamese dishes soon.

We are seeking an assertive, positive driven person to help launch our multi unit restaurant chain. Growth is leading us to soon be running a commissary kitchen off site and will be moving towards other locations within the next year.

Position Overview: The Kitchen Manager (KM) is responsible for all kitchen operations on an opening, mid- or closing shift, including supervision of all kitchen staff and staffing levels, safety and sanitation, housekeeping, and made from scratch food production related activities. The KM ensures that the shift is run in a smooth manner with a focus on “Quality” while attending to any unexpected problems or emergencies that may arise. The Manager is assigned a work group, Line Cooks, Pep Cooks or Dishwashers. The Manager is responsible for staffing, scheduling, financial goals and staff development of the assigned work group.

Key Duties & Responsibilities

People: The Kitchen Manager oversees kitchen operations during a shift and is responsible for about 6-8 staff within the kitchen. You will be expected to maintain and raise quality and morale of our kitchen staff through select hiring, scheduling, training, developing, mentoring, and managing. Owners strongly believe in being loyal to people and treating them exceptionally with a family like atmosphere that is professional yet, fun and emphasizes greater team work achieved through enjoying your relationships with your co workers.

Quality Profits: The KM has contributory financial responsibility for food costs, labor costs and kitchen supplies for the kitchen. Our KMs set operational goals and plans to achieve or exceed written budgets, then direct staff and utilizes kitchen systems, schedules, tools and procedures to attain those goals

Duties: Maintain food quality and safety standards, overseeing all phases of food procurement, production and service, including, inventory and ordering, storage and rotation, food preparation, recipe adherence, plate presentation, and service and production time standards.

Qualifications:

  • Must have 2 years of kitchen management
  • Experience in a wok based kitchen
  • Strong communication skills
  • Strong leadership skills
  • Speaking Spanish a plus
  • Culinary school background a plus
  • Ability to speak and understand Spanish a plus
  • Able to work ten hour-plus shifts plus ability to stand, sit or walk for extended periods of time

  • Must possess ability to discern product, taste, texture, temperature and presentation and preparation

Managers can expect to be treated very well having been in on the ground floor at this brands infancy. This will include profit bonuses & possible stock options. Owner is very generous and is seeking very committed individuals willing to get in at the ground level and build coherence among a team. He is very aggressive and is known for changing industries he competes in and has demonstrated that in the past. So if you are a can do kind of person that likes to build lasting relationships in a company where you are more than just a number on a P&L we would like to speak with you about this opportunity.
