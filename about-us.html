About Us...

At Savor Flavor Asia we strive to provide you the best Asian cuisine. We prepare our food fresh everyday. We have a variety of gourmet food to meet your expectations whether is Chinese, Japanese or Thai. From our delicious Teriyaki chicken to the mouth watering Pad Thai, we pledge to offer you the freshest food daily. Come to Savor Flavor Asia and experience the best Asian Cuisine in the valley!

Rod was planning this restaurant concept for the last 18 years. He lived in Asia for almost 4 years, both in Thailand and Vietnam. Rod also has extensively toured other countries such as Malaysia, Singapore, Indonesia, Hong Kong and China, to name a few, all gathering flavor profiles to eventually share with America in his new concept. Believe or not, he prefers Asian cuisine and eats it most every meal. Ngan has an incredible pallet and is known for being able to taste a dish once and make it twice and have a customer favorite on her hands! She brings a tremendous depth of traditional Vietnamese cooking knowledge with her passed down from generations in her family.

They came back to America to start a family and a new restaurant concept in Asian food. Now they own and operate Savor Flavor Asia, in Ahwatukee restaurant that has been an Asian restaurant of various different formats for nearly 20 years. Savor Flavor Asia, while it has a Japanese name currently, is mostly has a Chinese influenced menu with accents of Thai, Korean and a few Japanese dishes. Rod has hit the ground running with his beautiful wife by his side, sharing in this passion. Their next goal is to enhance the menu by adding Vietnamese, more Korean and a few other surprises.. They will be changing the name to a more appropriate eclectic name that better reflects what they will be sharing to their guests. That name is “Savor Flavor Asia”… They are very excited about this new venture and hope to shake your hand soon and let you share in their passion of experiencing fresh flavored dishes from all over Asia!
